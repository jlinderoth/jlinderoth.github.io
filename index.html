Workshop on Mixed Integer Programming

August 4-7, 2008
At Columbia University, New York City



Description

MIP-2008 is the fifth workshop in a series designed to bring the integer programming research community together, following the workshops

  • MIP 2003
  • MIP 2005
  • MIP 2006
  • MIP 2007

At MIP-2008, there will be a single track of invited talks. The program will be posted at a later date, but a list of tentative speakers can be found here. Anyone with interests in mixed integer programming is encouraged to attend the workshop.
