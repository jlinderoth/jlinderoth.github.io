Research Interests

My research interests lie in solving large-scale numerical optimization problems. Specific areas on which I have worked include

  • Mixed-Integer Linear Programming
  • Mixed-Integer Nonlinear Programming
  • Stochastic Programming
  • Grid Computing
  • High Performance Computing
  • Global Optimization
  • Applications: Energy, Logistics, Security, Engineering Design

My Google scholar profile page has more information about my papers and citations.
